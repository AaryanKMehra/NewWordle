# NewWordle

A playable word-guessing game built for the AI Webdev elective.

## How to Play
1. Guess the hidden 5-letter word in 6 tries.
2. Each guess must be a valid 5-letter word.
3. The color of the tiles will change to show how close your guess was:
   - **Black**: The letter is in the word and in the correct spot.
   - **Dark Grey**: The letter is in the word but in the wrong spot.
   - **Light Grey**: The letter is not in the word.
4. **Infinite Mode:** Every time you win, your score increases and you get a new word. If you lose, your score resets to 0.
